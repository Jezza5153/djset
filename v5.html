From NL
to BCN

From Harderwijk to Barcelona. PEAK took the Dutch drum & bass school south and built a scene around it — co-founding DnBabes, the collective putting women behind the decks of the city’s heaviest nights.

“Every set is a revolution — cut, weighted, and rolled at 174.”

First sets in January 2024. Since then: a homecoming at The Void festival in Utrecht, an Apologia × DnBabes night at Sala Apolo, and the Room 2 closing slot at Liquicity Barcelona — same night as Fox Stevenson, Maduk and Emily Makis. The circle keeps widening.

PEAK · co-founder, DnBabesDrum & Babes Barcelona — fierce, free and for everyone. Selector, promoter, community-builder; booked for high-energy rooms that want the tempo kept honest.
