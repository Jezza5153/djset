The story — NL → BCN

Dutch by birth. Barcelona by choice. PEAK came south for the light and stayed for the dark — the rooms after midnight where 174 starts to make sense.

Founder of DnB Babes BCN, the collective putting new names — and new energy — behind the decks of the city’s drum & bass scene.

Then Liquicity Barcelona: one bill with Fox Stevenson, Emily Makis and Maduk. Then Mallorca. Then Utrecht. The rooms keep getting bigger. The hour stays the same.
