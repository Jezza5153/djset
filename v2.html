02 — Story

FROM NL

TO BCN.

Dutch-born. Barcelona-based. PEAK landed in BCN in 2022 and started playing rooms the size of living rooms. Three years later the circuit knows the name — and the tempo never dropped below 174.

Founder of DnB Babes BCN — the collective putting women behind the decks of Barcelona's drum & bass scene and keeping them there.

Liquicity Barcelona — a lineup shared with Fox Stevenson, Emily Makis and Maduk. Then Mallorca. Then Utrecht. The run is still accelerating.
